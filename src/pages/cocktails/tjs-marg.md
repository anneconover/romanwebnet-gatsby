## Ingredients

- Casamigos Tequila
- Ice
- Trader Joe's Jalapeno Limeade

## Directions

### Step 1

Put ice and 2 shots of tequila in cup

### Step 2

Fill the rest of the cup with limeade

### Step 3

enjoy
